# Synthetic Sales Playbook

This is public-safe demo data for the offer demo.

Discovery calls should confirm budget, authority, need, timing, and a dated next step.
If the buyer raises a price concern, the manager should summarize the business value,
confirm who owns the decision, and send a short recap after human approval.

Follow-up messages must be specific:

- restate the problem in one sentence;
- mention the agreed next step;
- avoid inventing prices or promises;
- create a CRM task when any qualification signal is missing;
- route high-risk leads to a manager before updating the CRM stage.

AI output is a draft. A human reviewer approves or rejects the follow-up before the
CRM handoff is queued.
